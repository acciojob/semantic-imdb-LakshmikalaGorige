Summary

"Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film explores the concept of entering dreams to steal or plant ideas in a person's subconscious mind. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, is a skilled thief in the world of corporate espionage who specializes in this dangerous form of espionage. The movie takes the audience on a journey through layers of dreams within dreams, blurring the line between reality and the dream world.

Actors

"Inception" features a stellar cast, including:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Ellen Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
